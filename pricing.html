Decal.

A floor decal for a dance floor is a great way to add a unique and personalized touch to any event. Whether it's a wedding, birthday party, or corporate event, a custom floor decal can create a fun and memorable atmosphere. With a durable, non-slip surface, these decals are safe for guests to dance and walk on, and can be easily removed after the event without damaging the floor. Overall, a floor decal is a creative and eye-catching addition to any dance floor that is sure to impress guests and create a lasting impression.
